About Us

SATELIT was born out of an urgent concern to raise literacy and academic awareness among Indonesian students in Sakarya, Turkiye. We believe that by building a SOLİTTT intellectual community, we can create an environment that supports the personal and professional development of each member.

Our Projects

Satelit Magazine

Satelit Magazine serves as a comprehensive publication that compiles articles from both internal research and external writers. It acts as a platform to share insights, analyses, and thought-provoking pieces with the public. The magazine covers a wide range of topics, reflecting expertise and perspectives from various contributors. By bringing together internal research findings and external viewpoints, Satelit Magazine ensures a well-rounded and engaging reading experience, fostering intellectual discourse and knowledge sharing.

FORBES

FORBES is a platform dedicated to showcasing outstanding students in Sakarya and across Turkey. It highlights individuals not only for their academic excellence but also for their achievements in arts, sports, and various other fields. The initiative aims to recognize and celebrate young talent, providing them with the visibility they deserve. By featuring high-achieving students, FORBES inspires others to pursue excellence while also creating a network of role models for future generations.

Report Outlook

Report Outlook is a biannual report that provides a structured overview of the six-month progress within Satelit. It serves as a performance and impact assessment tool, detailing key developments, milestones, and achievements within the publication. By systematically analyzing the progress, Report Outlook ensures transparency, continuous improvement, and strategic planning for the future. It acts as a reflection tool that helps in evaluating the magazine's growth and effectiveness in delivering quality content.
